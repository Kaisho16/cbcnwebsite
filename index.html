Who We Are

Caloocan Bible Church North is a Bible-believing and Bible-centered evangelical church affiliated with ABCCOP.

We believe in the transformative power of God's Word and are committed to making disciples who love God and love others. Our doors are open to everyone seeking community and spiritual growth.
