About Us

Discover our mission, values, and what we believe.

Caloocan Bible Church North is under the umbrella of Evangelical Church with its affiliation on Alliance of Bible Christian Churches of the Philippines (ABCCOP), while its international counterpart is World Evangelical Association (WEA). As an evangelical church, CBCN is Bible-believing & Bible-centered church were the Bible is the primary and sole authority of faith and practice.
