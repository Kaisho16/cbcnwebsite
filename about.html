Our Story

Caloocan Bible Church North has been serving the community since 2009, bringing the message of hope and faith to families in Caloocan City.

We are an evangelical church under the umbrella of the Alliance of Bible Christian Churches of the Philippines (ABCCOP), with our international counterpart being the World Evangelical Association (WEA).

As a Bible-believing and Bible-centered church, we hold the Scripture as the primary and sole authority of faith and practice. Our doors are open to everyone seeking spiritual growth, community, and a deeper relationship with God.

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who will love God, love others, and make a difference in the world through the power of the Gospel.
